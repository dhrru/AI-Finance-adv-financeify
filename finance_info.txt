The 50/30/20 rule: Allocate 50% of income to Needs, 30% to Wants, and 20% to Savings.
Emergency Fund: You should save at least 3 to 6 months of living expenses.
Debt Snowball: Pay off the smallest debts first to build momentum.
Compound Interest: The process where the value of an investment increases because the earnings on an investment earn interest as time passes.
